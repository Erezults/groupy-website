Accessibility Statement

Groupy Calendar

Last updated: July 14, 2026

The Groupy Calendar website ("the Site") is committed to providing equal and convenient access to its information and services for all users, including people with disabilities. We aim to conform to Israeli Standard 5568 at level AA (broadly aligned with the international WCAG 2.0/2.1 guidelines), pursuant to Israel's Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Law, 1998, and its accessibility regulations.

Accessibility Features on This Site

  • Dedicated accessibility widget — a floating button on every page lets you increase or decrease text size, switch to high contrast, and stop animations. Your preferences are remembered on future visits.
  • Full keyboard navigation — every link, button and field on the site is reachable and operable using the Tab key, including a "skip to content" link at the top of each page and a clearly visible focus outline.
  • Alt text — all meaningful images on the site have descriptive alternative text for screen readers.
  • Color contrast — text and background colors have been reviewed and adjusted to meet a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text.
  • Reduced-motion support — users with a system-level "reduce motion" preference, and users who enable "stop animations" in the accessibility widget, get a site free of non-essential animation.
  • Semantic structure — headings, navigation and content regions use semantic HTML and ARIA labels to support screen readers.

Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, some parts of the site may not yet be fully conformant. We continue to review and improve accessibility on an ongoing basis. If you encounter an accessibility issue, please let us know using the contact details below.
