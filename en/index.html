Features

Everything you need to coordinate — without the chaos

Add an event once, and everyone in the group sees it instantly. Anyone can RSVP, comment, and get a reminder right on time.

Shared group calendar

One event, visible to your whole group, with a color per member — so it's always clear what belongs to whom.

RSVP & comments

RSVP and a conversation under every event — everyone knows who's coming, with no endless threads.

Recurring events

Daily, weekly, monthly or yearly — set it once and the event repeats on its own.

Push reminders

Remind a specific member or the whole group — right on time, automatically.

Smart summaries

Daily & weekly digests plus a comments summary — delivered at the hour you choose.

Payment tracking

An interactive monthly report: tap to mark who paid and who still owes — no spreadsheets.

Home-screen widgets

"Today" and "Month" in three sizes, with member colors — your next events always in view.

Birthdays

A yearly birthday board with reminders — you'll never forget anyone in the group.

Multiple groups & export

Manage several groups and switch between them easily, and export your calendar to ‎.ics‎ anytime.

New · AI

Turn text, a photo or your voice
into an event — automatically

Type a simple sentence like "Michal has a soccer game at the home field at 8pm", snap a photo of an invitation, or just record yourself — and Groupy's AI fills in the event details for you: date, time, members and title.

  • Plain text → a full event in one tap
  • A photo of an invite or screenshot
  • A voice recording in Hebrew or English
Payment tracking

Who paid and who still owes — crystal clear

Perfect for any activity that involves costs: classes, camps, training groups or a building committee. The interactive monthly report shows exactly who paid and who still owes — mark a payment with a single tap, no spreadsheets.

  • Mark "paid" with one tap
  • Monthly summary for the whole group
