General

Groupy is a clear, simple shared calendar for any group — a family, a class, a parents' committee, a sports team, or any community that needs to coordinate events in one place. Add an event once, and everyone in the group sees it instantly, can RSVP, comment, and get a reminder right on time.

Any group that coordinates events together: families, classes, camps, training groups, building committees and communities. Groupy is especially handy for activities that involve costs — so it's always clear who has paid and who still owes, without spreadsheets.

Groupy is available for iPhone, iPad and Mac. Everything syncs automatically across your devices, so you always see the same up-to-date calendar.

Yes. Groupy fully supports Hebrew and English, including a right-to-left (RTL) interface in Hebrew. You can switch languages anytime in Settings.

Using the app

Tap the day you want or the add button, fill in a title, date and time, and choose which members are involved — that's it. You can also set a recurrence (daily/weekly/monthly/yearly), a reminder, and a cost for the event.

The new-event screen has an "auto-fill with AI" field. You can type a plain sentence (e.g. "Michal has a soccer game at the home field at 8pm"), snap a photo of an invitation or a screenshot, or record yourself — and the AI fills in the event details for you: title, date, time and members. You can always review and edit before saving.

The group manager can add members and share the group. Each member gets access to the shared calendar, and you can give each member their own color so it's always clear what belongs to whom.

Members with edit access can add and edit events and use the AI features. Read-only members can view the calendar, RSVP and comment — but don't edit. This lets you share the calendar with many people while granting edit rights only to those who need them.

Yes. You can create and manage several groups (for example "Family", "Soccer club" and "Building committee") and switch between them easily. Unlimited groups are available with a Premium subscription.

Yes. You can view your calendar and add events even while offline, and everything syncs automatically once you're back online.

You can add a cost to any event and per member. The interactive monthly report brings it all together and shows who paid and who still owes — marking "paid" takes a single tap. You can also export your calendar to a ‎.ics‎ file.

Premium subscription

You can start right away with the Basic plan, which includes one group with up to 2 members, a shared calendar with full sync, and a monthly taste of AI. A Premium subscription unlocks unlimited members, expanded AI, multiple groups and more.

Premium includes: unlimited members in your group, up to 50 members with edit & AI access, unlimited read-only members, creating events from text/photo/voice, unlimited groups, and smart reports for the whole group.

The subscription is user-level — a single purchase unlocks all features across all of your groups.

Exact prices are always shown inside the app and on the App Store product page, in your country and currency. That way you see the up-to-date price before any purchase. View on the App Store →

It's a one-time purchase that unlocks all Premium features with no monthly subscription and no automatic renewal. It's currently offered as a limited founder offer. You can see availability and the price inside the app.

Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled. You can manage or cancel anytime in your Apple ID subscription settings (iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions), or from the Settings screen in the app.

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase, and on the subscription screen in the app tap "Restore purchases". All your features will come back automatically.

Account & privacy

Your data is stored securely and is used only to power your shared calendar. Features like Sign in with Apple, push notifications and ‎.ics‎ export are standard iOS capabilities, used only with your consent. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
